What is RoomMagnet?

Why Choose RoomMagnet?

Our goal is to bring professionals and semi-professionals together
with hosts who have extra room to share.

Finding Affordable
Housing Made Easy

Costs for college students, interns and young professionals continues to escalate at an alarming pace.

RoomMagnet helps you find an inexpensive, comfortable place to stay in the midst of a progessive lifestyle.

Tenants can even do light housekeeping and chores in exchange for reduced rent

Share your World,
Share your Space

Fill your empty nest and earn an income from the comfort of your own home.

RoomMagnet hosts develop lasting friendships, helping each other through the sharing economy.

Recieve assistance with light domestic duties such as shopping, household duties, daily maintenance or just companionship.
